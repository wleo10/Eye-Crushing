Have you ever played with the mod **End Remastered**, opened the end portal and the remaining eyes became useless?
That's why I made this small datapack which adds **Create** crushing recipes for these eyes, so that you can still make use of them.

## Results

The resources you get are usually a **vanilla eye of ender**, some **ender pearls**, **items with a similiar theme as the eye** like irom from the Lost Eye (found in mineshafts) and an **enchanted book with the enchantment level 1 higher than the vanilla limit**!

You also have a chance to get an **ender pearl** and **blaze powder** from a vanilla eye of ender.

![The image shows a Create crushing recipe of the Lost Eye (found in mineshafts) from End Remastered with an vanilla eye of ender, some ender pearls, an enchanted book and some underground valuables (iron, redstone dust and diamonds) as results](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/a0426ddd8bccb80da98ae64674bca9fa574c5ca9.png)

## Dependencies

- [Create](https://modrinth.com/mod/create) - the crushing wheels
- [End Remastered](https://modrinth.com/mod/endrem) - the eyes
